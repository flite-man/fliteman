Money matters

What's the first thing you do when your salary is credited?

You pay your bills. You pay your rent or your EMI's. You buy stuff. You go out to eat and drink.

But you almost never think of profitting from it. Yes, profit. Making money.

So how do you do it? Next time your salary is credited, take one or all of these smart moves:

- Transfer 10% to a savings account with a higher interest rate.
Don't have one? Open it. Already have one? Great! Leave the money in there, follow the next step instead.

- Transfer 10% - 20% into a recurring deposit at a higher interest rate than your savings account.

- Add another 10% to a SIP of your choice.

This simple 3 step model allocates 30% of your salary to making money from money. Profit.
